## FINAL RECOMMENDATIONS

1. **DO NOT LAUNCH** without completing Phase 1 and engaging legal counsel
2. **START WITH ONE JURISDICTION** before expanding internationally
3. **PRIORITIZE MINOR PROTECTIONS** given the target user demographic
4. **PLAN FOR AI COMPLIANCE** especially under EU AI Act
5. **BUDGET FOR LEGAL COUNSEL** in each target jurisdiction ($15,000-$50,000 per jurisdiction)
6. **PLAN FOR ONGOING COMPLIANCE** as laws evolve (budget $5,000-$15,000/month for legal monitoring)
7. **IMPLEMENT COMPLIANCE AS CODE** where possible (automated consent recording, automated deletion, automated audit logging)
8. **CREATE A COMPLIANCE TEAM** with dedicated resources for ongoing compliance management
9. **PLAN FOR REGULATORY CHANGES** by implementing version-controlled legal documents and re-consent workflows
10. **DOCUMENT EVERYTHING** — compliance is as much about documentation as it is about implementation
