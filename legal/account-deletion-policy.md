# COLLEGEOS — ACCOUNT DELETION AND DATA DELETION POLICY

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

## 1. PURPOSE

1.1 This Account Deletion and Data Deletion Policy ("Policy") describes how CollegeOS ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") handles account deletion and data deletion requests.

1.2 This Policy implements our commitments under our Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and applicable data protection laws.

1.3 This Policy applies to all deletion requests from users, parents/guardians of minor users, and legal representatives.

## 2. TYPES OF DELETION

### 2.1 Account Deletion

Account deletion permanently closes your CollegeOS account and initiates the deletion of your personal information.

### 2.2 Partial Data Deletion

You may request deletion of specific data categories (e.g., essays, documents, college list) without deleting your entire account.

### 2.3 Anonymization

In certain cases, we may anonymize your data rather than delete it, removing all identifiers so the data can no longer be attributed to you.

## 3. USER-REQUESTED DELETION

### 3.1 How to Request Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and data by:
   (a) Using the account deletion feature in your account settings at any time;
   (b) Emailing us at [PRIVACY EMAIL] with the subject line "Account Deletion Request";
   (c) Mailing a written request to [PRIVACY ADDRESS] with the subject "Account Deletion Request";
   (d) Contacting customer support and requesting account deletion.

### 3.2 Verification Process

To protect your account, we will verify your identity before processing a deletion request:
   (a) We will send a verification email to your registered email address;
   (b) You must click the verification link within 7 days;
   (c) If you cannot access your registered email, you must provide:
       - The email address associated with the account;
       - Your full name;
       - Approximate account creation date;
       - Any other information we request to verify your identity.

### 3.3 Deletion Confirmation

After verification, we will:
   (a) Send a confirmation email to your registered address;
   (b) Provide a summary of what data will be deleted;
   (c) Provide the expected deletion timeline;
   (d) Ask you to confirm you want to proceed with deletion.

### 3.4 Deletion Timeline

   (a) **Immediate Actions:** Account access is disabled immediately upon confirmation;
   (b) **30-Day Period:** Your data is deleted or anonymized within 30 days of confirmation;
   (c) **Confirmation of Completion:** You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete;
   (d) **Backup Systems:** Data in backup systems will be deleted when backup cycles expire (up to 90 days).

### 3.5 What Is Deleted

Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize:
   (a) Account information (name, email, phone, date of birth);
   (b) Academic profile information;
   (c) Extracurricular and activity information;
   (d) Financial information;
   (e) Application tracker data;
   (f) User-generated content (essays, notes, comments);
   (g) Uploaded documents and files;
   (h) Usage data and analytics (individual-level);
   (i) Cookie data and preferences;
   (j) Communication records (support messages, emails).

### 3.6 What May Not Be Immediately Deleted

We may retain certain information despite deletion requests:
   (a) **Legal Compliance Data:** Terms of Service acceptances, Privacy Policy acceptances, cookie consent records (retained for 7 years);
   (b) **Fraud Prevention Data:** Information needed to prevent fraud (retained for 7 years);
   (c) **Aggregated Data:** Anonymized and aggregated data that cannot identify you;
   (d) **Legal Disputes:** Data relevant to ongoing disputes or claims;
   (e) **Backup Systems:** Data in backup systems until backup cycles expire (up to 90 days);
   (f) **AI Training Data:** Data already incorporated into trained AI models (anonymized and aggregated);
   (g) **Payment Records:** Transaction records retained for tax purposes (7 years).

### 3.7 Irreversibility

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. After deletion:
   (a) You cannot recover your account or data;
   (b) You cannot recreate the account with the same email;
   (c) All recommendations, college lists, and application data are permanently lost;
   (d) You must create a new account to use the Service again.

## 4. PARENT-REQUESTED DELETION

### 4.1 Who May Request

A Parent or Guardian may request deletion of their Minor User's account and data.

### 4.2 How to Request

Parents and Guardians may request deletion by:
   (a) Using the account deletion feature (with Minor User's access);
   (b) Emailing [MINOR SUPPORT EMAIL] with the subject "Minor User Deletion Request";
   (c) Mailing a written request to [MINOR SUPPORT ADDRESS];
   (d) Contacting customer support and identifying themselves as the Parent or Guardian.

### 4.3 Verification Process

For parent deletion requests, we will verify:
   (a) The requester's identity (government-issued ID or equivalent);
   (b) The requester's relationship to the Minor User (birth certificate, school records, or other reasonable proof);
   (c) The Minor User's account details (email, name, approximate creation date);
   (d) Written consent from the Minor User (if age-appropriate, typically 14+).

### 4.4 Deletion Timeline

Same as user-requested deletion (Section 3.4):
   (a) Immediate: Account access disabled;
   (b) 30 days: Data deleted or anonymized;
   (c) Confirmation: Written confirmation sent to Parent or Guardian.

### 4.5 Minor User Notification

If the Minor User is 14 years or older, we will:
   (a) Notify the Minor User of the deletion request;
   (b) Provide an opportunity for the Minor User to respond;
   (c) Consider the Minor User's wishes (though the Parent's decision is ultimately binding);
   (d) Explain the consequences of deletion to the Minor User.

## 5. DELETION WORKFLOW

### 5.1 Step 1: Request Received

   (a) Deletion request is received via any channel;
   (b) Request is logged in the data_deletion_requests table;
   (c) Request ID is generated and provided to the requester;
   (d) Acknowledgment is sent within 24 hours.

### 5.2 Step 2: Identity Verification

   (a) Identity verification is initiated;
   (b) Verification email or document request is sent;
   (c) Requester has 7 days to complete verification;
   (d) If verification fails, requester is notified and given 14 days to resubmit.

### 5.3 Step 3: Confirmation

   (a) Upon successful verification, deletion confirmation is sent;
   (b) Summary of what will be deleted is provided;
   (c) Requester must confirm they want to proceed;
   (d) Confirmation must be provided within 14 days or request expires.

### 5.4 Step 4: Deletion Execution

   (a) Account access is immediately disabled;
   (b) Personal data is deleted from primary systems within 30 days;
   (c) User-generated content is deleted;
   (d) Documents and files are permanently deleted;
   (e) Usage data is anonymized;
   (f) Legal compliance data is retained per retention policy;
   (g) Backup data will expire per backup rotation schedule.

### 5.5 Step 5: Confirmation of Completion

   (a) Deletion confirmation is sent within 5 business days of completion;
   (b) Confirmation includes:
       - Date of deletion;
       - Categories of data deleted;
       - Categories of data retained (if any);
       - Confirmation that deletion is complete;
       - Contact information for follow-up questions.

### 5.6 Step 6: Record Retention

   (a) Deletion request record is retained for 7 years (legal compliance);
   (b) Deletion confirmation is retained for 7 years;
   (c) Verification documents are retained for 1 year, then deleted;
   (d) All records are stored securely and access is restricted.

## 6. EXCEPTIONS TO DELETION

### 6.1 Legal Requirements

We may refuse or delay deletion when required by:
   (a) Applicable law or regulation;
   (b) Court order or governmental request;
   (c) Tax or accounting requirements;
   (d) Anti-money laundering requirements;
   (e) Child protection obligations.

### 6.2 Ongoing Disputes

We may retain data relevant to:
   (a) Ongoing legal disputes;
   (b) Pending claims or complaints;
   (c) Fraud investigations;
   (d) Security incidents.

### 6.3 Technical Limitations

In rare cases, technical limitations may prevent immediate deletion:
   (a) Distributed systems requiring synchronization;
   (b) Third-party service providers with different deletion processes;
   (c) Backup systems on extended retention cycles.

In such cases, we will:
   (a) Notify the requester of the limitation;
   (b) Provide an estimated deletion timeline;
   (c) Prioritize deletion of the affected data.

## 7. COMPLIANCE RETENTION

### 7.1 Tax Records

Payment and transaction records will be retained for 7 years (or longer if required by applicable tax law).

### 7.2 Legal Claims

Data relevant to potential or ongoing legal claims will be retained until:
   (a) The claim is resolved;
   (b) The statute of limitations expires;
   (c) Legal obligation to retain expires.

### 7.3 Fraud Prevention

Information needed to prevent fraud will be retained for 7 years from the date of the fraudulent activity or account termination.

### 7.4 Regulatory Requirements

Data required to be retained by regulatory authorities will be retained for the period specified by the applicable regulation.

## 8. DATA EXPORT BEFORE DELETION

### 8.1 Right to Export

Before deleting your account, you have the right to export your data:
   (a) Request a copy of all your personal data;
   (b) Receive data in a machine-readable format (JSON, CSV);
   (c) Receive a comprehensive export of your Academic Profile, college list, application data, and communications;
   (d) Have 30 days to export data after deletion confirmation.

### 8.2 How to Request Export

   (a) Use the data export feature in account settings;
   (b) Email [PRIVACY EMAIL] with subject "Data Export Request";
   (c) We will prepare the export within 30 days;
   (d) Export will be delivered via secure download link.

## 9. DELETION VERIFICATION

### 9.1 Post-Deletion Verification

After deletion, you may verify that your data has been deleted by:
   (a) Attempting to log in with your old credentials (should fail);
   (b) Requesting a data access report (should return empty);
   (c) Contacting us with your deletion confirmation ID.

### 9.2 Dispute Resolution

If you believe your data was not properly deleted:
   (a) Contact us at [PRIVACY EMAIL];
   (b) Provide your deletion confirmation ID;
   (c) We will investigate within 15 business days;
   (d) We will take corrective action if deletion was incomplete.
