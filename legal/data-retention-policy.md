# COLLEGEOS — DATA RETENTION POLICY

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

## 1. PURPOSE

1.1 This Data Retention Policy ("Policy") defines what personal information CollegeOS ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") stores, why it is stored, how long it is stored, and when it is deleted.

1.2 This Policy implements our commitments under our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

1.3 This Policy applies to all personal information processed by CollegeOS, regardless of format or storage location.

## 2. DATA CATEGORIES AND RETENTION PERIODS

### 2.1 Account Information

   (a) **Data Stored:**
       - Full name
       - Email address
       - Password hash (encrypted)
       - Phone number (if provided)
       - Date of birth (for age verification)
       - Country of residence
       - Account creation date
       - Last login date
       - Account status (active, suspended, deleted)

   (b) **Why Stored:** Account creation, authentication, age verification, communication, security.

   (c) **Retention Period:**
       - Active accounts: Indefinitely, while account remains active;
       - Inactive accounts (no login for 24 months): 12 months after last login, then deletion;
       - Deleted accounts: 30 days after deletion request, then permanent deletion;
       - Suspended accounts: Until suspension is lifted or permanently, depending on reason.

   (d) **Deletion Schedule:** Monthly cleanup of expired inactive accounts.

### 2.2 Academic Profile Information

   (a) **Data Stored:**
       - GPA, percentage scores, class rank
       - Standardized test scores (SAT, ACT, IB, AP)
       - Intended major(s)
       - Degree level preference
       - School name and type
       - Academic strengths and achievements
       - Honors and awards

   (b) **Why Stored:** Generate recommendations, calculate admissions chances, provide analytics.

   (c) **Retention Period:**
       - Active accounts: Indefinitely, while account remains active;
       - Deleted accounts: 30 days after deletion request;
       - Aggregated/anonymized data: Indefinitely (no longer identifiable).

   (d) **Deletion Schedule:** Processed with account deletion workflow.

### 2.3 Extracurricular and Activity Information

   (a) **Data Stored:**
       - Extracurricular activities
       - Leadership roles
       - Community service
       - Internships and work experience
       - Research projects
       - Publications
       - Competitions and awards
       - Sports and arts activities

   (b) **Why Stored:** Generate recommendations, assess profile strength, provide analytics.

   (c) **Retention Period:** Same as Academic Profile Information (Section 2.2).

### 2.4 Financial Information

   (a) **Data Stored:**
       - Family income (if provided)
       - Maximum budget for education
       - Financial aid needs
       - Scholarship interest
       - Loan eligibility preferences

   (b) **Why Stored:** Generate financial recommendations, assess affordability, provide financial intelligence.

   (c) **Retention Period:**
       - Active accounts: Indefinitely, while account remains active;
       - Deleted accounts: 30 days after deletion request;
       - Payment transaction data: 7 years (tax and legal compliance).

   (d) **Deletion Schedule:** Processed with account deletion workflow. Payment data retained per tax requirements.

### 2.5 Application Tracker Data

   (a) **Data Stored:**
       - College list (colleges saved, tracked)
       - Application status
       - Deadlines
       - Application documents
       - Decision tracking
       - Notes and comments

   (b) **Why Stored:** Provide application tracking functionality.

   (c) **Retention Period:**
       - Active accounts: Indefinitely, while account remains active;
       - Deleted accounts: 30 days after deletion request;
       - Application documents: 30 days after deletion request, then permanent deletion.

   (d) **Deletion Schedule:** Processed with account deletion workflow.

### 2.6 User-Generated Content

   (a) **Data Stored:**
       - Essays and personal statements
       - Notes and comments
       - Feedback and suggestions
       - Feature requests
       - Reports and complaints

   (b) **Why Stored:** Provide Service functionality, improve Service, respond to inquiries.

   (c) **Retention Period:**
       - Essays and personal statements: 30 days after deletion request;
       - Notes and comments: 30 days after deletion request;
       - Feedback and suggestions: 24 months after submission (for improvement analysis), then anonymized;
       - Feature requests: 24 months after submission, then anonymized;
       - Reports and complaints: 36 months after resolution (for dispute resolution).

   (d) **Deletion Schedule:** Processed with account deletion workflow.

### 2.7 Documents and Files

   (a) **Data Stored:**
       - Transcripts and academic records
       - Essays and personal statements (uploaded files)
       - Recommendation letters
       - Financial documents
       - Certificates and awards
       - Portfolio materials

   (b) **Why Stored:** Enable document upload features, support application tracking.

   (c) **Retention Period:**
       - Active accounts: Indefinitely, while account remains active;
       - Deleted accounts: 30 days after deletion request;
       - Temporary uploads: 24 hours after upload if not saved to account.

   (d) **Deletion Schedule:** Processed with account deletion workflow. Files permanently deleted after 30 days.

### 2.8 Communications

   (a) **Data Stored:**
       - Customer support messages
       - Email correspondence
       - Survey responses
       - Bug reports
       - Error logs

   (b) **Why Stored:** Provide customer support, improve Service, debug issues.

   (c) **Retention Period:**
       - Support messages: 24 months after resolution;
       - Email correspondence: 24 months after last correspondence;
       - Survey responses: 12 months after submission, then anonymized;
       - Bug reports: 12 months after resolution, then anonymized;
       - Error logs: 90 days (for debugging purposes).

   (d) **Deletion Schedule:** Quarterly cleanup of expired communications.

### 2.9 Usage Data and Analytics

   (a) **Data Stored:**
       - Page views and feature usage
       - Click patterns and navigation
       - Search queries
       - Session duration
       - Device information
       - IP addresses
       - Cookie data

   (b) **Why Stored:** Analyze usage, improve Service, measure performance.

   (c) **Retention Period:**
       - Raw usage data: 12 months;
       - Aggregated analytics: Indefinitely (anonymized);
       - IP addresses: 90 days (then anonymized);
       - Session data: 90 days;
       - Cookie consent records: 12 months.

   (d) **Deletion Schedule:** Monthly cleanup of raw usage data. Quarterly anonymization of IP addresses.

### 2.10 AI Model Training Data

   (a) **Data Stored:**
       - Anonymized and aggregated user data used for AI training
       - Model version history
       - Training dataset metadata

   (b) **Why Stored:** Train, improve, and validate AI models.

   (c) **Retention Period:**
       - Training datasets: Until model is retrained (typically 12-24 months);
       - Model versions: 36 months after retirement;
       - Training metadata: Indefinitely (for audit purposes).

   (d) **Deletion Schedule:** Processed when models are retrained. Retired models deleted after 36 months.

### 2.11 Legal and Compliance Data

   (a) **Data Stored:**
       - Terms of Service acceptance records
       - Privacy Policy acceptance records
       - Cookie consent records
       - Data deletion requests
       - Parental requests
       - Legal disputes and claims

   (b) **Why Stored:** Legal compliance, dispute resolution, audit trails.

   (c) **Retention Period:**
       - Acceptance records: 7 years after last account activity;
       - Deletion requests: 7 years after resolution;
       - Parental requests: 7 years after resolution;
       - Legal disputes: 7 years after resolution;
       - Tax records: 7 years (or longer if required by law);
       - Fraud prevention records: 7 years.

   (d) **Deletion Schedule:** Annual review of compliance data. Deleted when retention period expires.

### 2.12 Backup Data

   (a) **Data Stored:** Full system backups including all categories above.

   (b) **Why Stored:** Disaster recovery, data protection.

   (c) **Retention Period:**
       - Daily backups: 30 days;
       - Weekly backups: 90 days;
       - Monthly backups: 1 year;
       - Yearly backups: 3 years.

   (d) **Deletion Schedule:** Automated rotation. Backups older than retention period automatically deleted.

## 3. RETENTION PRINCIPLES

3.1 **Storage Limitation.** We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.

3.2 **Purpose Limitation.** Personal information will not be retained longer than necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

3.3 **Data Minimization.** We collect and retain only the personal information that is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary.

3.4 **Accuracy.** We take reasonable steps to ensure personal information is accurate and, where necessary, kept up to date.

3.5 **Security.** We implement appropriate security measures to protect personal information during the retention period.

## 4. EXCEPTIONS TO RETENTION PERIODS

4.1 **Legal Holds.** If retention is required by law, regulation, or legal process, we will retain information beyond the standard retention period until the obligation is satisfied.

4.2 **Ongoing Disputes.** If information is relevant to an ongoing dispute, claim, or investigation, we will retain it until the matter is resolved.

4.3 **Fraud Prevention.** If information is needed for fraud prevention or security purposes, we will retain it for as long as necessary to prevent fraud.

4.4 **Aggregated Data.** Aggregated or anonymized data that cannot reasonably be used to identify an individual may be retained indefinitely for analytics and research.

## 5. REVIEW AND UPDATES

5.1 **Annual Review.** This Policy will be reviewed annually to ensure retention periods remain appropriate.

5.2 **Legal Changes.** Retention periods will be updated if legal requirements change.

5.3 **Notification.** Material changes to this Policy will be communicated to users via email and website notice.
