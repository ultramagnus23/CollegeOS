# COLLEGEOS — MINOR USER POLICY

Last Updated: June 20, 2026

## 1. PURPOSE

1.1 This Minor User Policy ("Policy") establishes additional protections for users aged 13-17 ("Minor Users") who use the CollegeOS platform ("Service").

1.2 This Policy supplements our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. By permitting a Minor User to use the Service, you acknowledge and agree to this Policy.

## 2. AGE REQUIREMENTS

2.1 **Minimum Age.** The Service is available only to users aged 13 and above. Users under 13 are strictly prohibited.

2.2 **Minor User Definition.** A "Minor User" is any user who is at least 13 years old but under 18 years of age at the time of account creation or use.

2.3 **Age Verification.** We may implement age verification measures, including:
   (a) Self-declaration of age during signup;
   (b) Request for proof of age in certain circumstances;
   (c) Third-party age verification services;
   (d) Behavioral indicators suggesting underage use.

2.4 **Age Misrepresentation.** Providing false age information is a violation of our Terms of Service and may result in account termination.

## 3. PARENT/GUARDIAN INVOLVEMENT

3.1 **Required Involvement.** Minor Users must use the Service with the involvement, consent, and supervision of a Parent or Guardian.

3.2 **Parent/Guardian Consent.** By permitting a Minor User to use the Service, a Parent or Guardian:
   (a) Confirms they have reviewed this Policy and our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy;
   (b) Consent to the collection, use, and processing of the Minor User's personal information;
   (c) Agree to be bound by these Terms on behalf of the Minor User;
   (d) Accept responsibility for the Minor User's use of the Service;
   (e) Acknowledge they may exercise data subject rights on behalf of the Minor User.

3.3 **Ongoing Supervision.** Parents and Guardians should:
   (a) Regularly review the Minor User's activity on the Service;
   (b) Discuss college planning and admissions with the Minor User;
   (c) Verify information provided through the Service;
   (d) Monitor any documents or sensitive information uploaded;
   (e) Be aware of the AI disclaimer and limitations of AI-generated outputs.

3.4 **Parent Access.** Parents and Guardians have the right to:
   (a) Access the Minor User's account with the Minor User's knowledge;
   (b) Review all information the Minor User has provided;
   (c) Review all information collected about the Minor User;
   (d) Modify or correct the Minor User's information;
   (e) Request deletion of the Minor User's account and data.

## 4. DATA COLLECTION FOR MINOR USERS

4.1 **Enhanced Disclosure.** When collecting information from Minor Users, we provide:
   (a) Clear, age-appropriate language explaining what data is collected;
   (b) Explanation of why the data is collected;
   (c) Explanation of how the data will be used;
   (d) Notice of parental rights regarding the Minor User's data.

4.2 **Educational Profile Data.** For Minor Users, we collect:
   (a) Academic information (grades, test scores, GPA);
   (b) Intended majors and career interests;
   (c) Extracurricular activities and achievements;
   (d) College preferences and priorities;
   (e) Financial information (with parental awareness).

4.3 **Sensitive Data Protections.** For Minor Users, we apply additional protections to:
   (a) Academic records and transcripts;
   (b) Essays and personal statements;
   (c) Financial information;
   (d) Location information;
   (e) Any information that could identify the Minor User's school.

4.4 **Document Upload Restrictions.** Minor Users uploading documents must have parental awareness when uploading:
   (a) Transcripts and academic records;
   (b) Financial documents;
   (c) Identity documents;
   (d) Medical or health information (which we do not collect).

## 5. EDUCATIONAL INFORMATION HANDLING

5.1 **Academic Data Purpose.** Academic information from Minor Users is used solely to:
   (a) Generate personalized college recommendations;
   (b) Calculate admissions chances;
   (c) Provide profile strength assessments;
   (d) Improve our AI models (anonymized and aggregated);
   (e) Not for any purpose unrelated to educational planning.

5.2 **Data Minimization.** We collect only the educational information necessary to provide the Service:
   (a) We do not require unnecessary academic details;
   (b) We allow partial or approximate information;
   (c) We do not require official transcripts for basic features;
   (d) We do not share academic information with colleges without explicit consent.

5.3 **Accuracy Responsibility.** Minor Users and their Parents/Guardians are responsible for:
   (a) Providing accurate academic information;
   (b) Understanding that inaccurate information leads to inaccurate recommendations;
   (c) Not fabricating or falsifying academic achievements;
   (d) Correcting information if it changes.

## 8. SAFETY PROVISIONS

8.1 **Content Moderation.** We implement additional content moderation for Minor Users:
   (a) Screening of user-generated content for appropriateness;
   (b) Prohibition on sharing personal contact information;
   (c) Monitoring for signs of distress or concerning behavior;
   (d) Reporting mechanisms for concerns.

8.2 **Communication Restrictions.** We restrict Minor Users from:
   (a) Sharing personal contact information with other users;
   (b) Initiating private messaging with other users;
   (c) Sharing location information;
   (d) Sharing information that could identify their school or location.

8.3 **Reporting Concerns.** If we identify concerns about a Minor User's safety, we may:
   (a) Notify the Parent or Guardian;
   (b) Suspend the account pending investigation;
   (c) Report to appropriate authorities if required by law;
   (d) Provide resources and support information.

8.4 **Crisis Resources.** We may provide crisis resources to Minor Users who appear to be in distress, including:
   (a) Mental health hotlines;
   (b) Crisis text lines;
   (c) Educational counseling resources;
   (d) School counselor contact information.
