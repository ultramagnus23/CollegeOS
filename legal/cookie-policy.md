## 2. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

2.1 **Definition.** Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, maintain your session, and understand how you use the site.

2.2 **Similar Technologies.** We may also use:
   (a) Web beacons (pixel tags);
   (b) Local storage (HTML5);
   (c) Session storage;
   (d) Fingerprints;
   (e) Similar tracking technologies.

## 5. COOKIE MANAGEMENT

### 5.1 Cookie Consent Manager

We provide a Cookie Consent Manager that allows you to:
   (a) Accept all cookies;
   (b) Reject non-essential cookies;
   (c) Customize your cookie preferences by category;
   (d) Withdraw consent at any time.

Access the Cookie Consent Manager from:
   (a) The cookie banner on first visit;
   (b) The footer of every page;
   (c) Your account settings.

### 5.2 Browser Controls

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

   (a) **Google Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies;
   (b) **Mozilla Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies;
   (c) **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies;
   (d) **Microsoft Edge:** Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions;
   (e) **Other Browsers:** Consult your browser's help documentation.

### 5.3 Opt-Out Tools

You can use these tools to opt out of certain tracking:
   (a) Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
   (b) Network Advertising Initiative Opt-out: https://optout.networkadvertising.org
   (c) Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-out: https://optout.aboutads.info

### 5.4 Consequences of Blocking Cookies

If you block or delete cookies:
   (a) Essential cookies cannot be blocked without breaking the Service;
   (b) Non-essential cookies may be blocked, but this may affect functionality;
   (c) Your preferences may not be remembered;
   (d) You may need to log in more frequently.

## 6. COOKIE LIFESPAN

6.1 **Session Cookies.** These cookies expire when you close your browser. They are used for:
   (a) Authentication sessions;
   (b) Temporary data storage;
   (c) Security verification.

6.2 **Persistent Cookies.** These cookies remain on your device for a specified period:
   (a) Preference cookies: 365 days;
   (b) Analytics cookies: 24 months;
   (c) Authentication tokens: Varies by security policy;
   (d) Cookie consent records: 12 months.
